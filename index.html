About Us

What we offer:

We offer high-quality EUP, 4K liveries, and unique scripts that are being slowly developed. My main focus is making unique scripts for FiveM, and maybe some other games in the future if suggested by the community. We provide 4K liveries and custom EUP re-texturing free of charge.

About me & Our Mission as a Team:

I am a solo developer for FiveM with basic programming experience in Python, Lua, and JavaScript. My goal is to create scripts, liveries, and EUP packs that anyone can use and enjoy—respecting both design ideas and allowing for full customization where possible.

I started unofficially back in 2023, without a Discord, just sharing mods with friends on various platforms, including Minecraft and Roblox mods, as well as smaller games. I began making scripts, liveries, and EUP for FiveM in 2024-2025 and have previous coding experience in Lua, Java, Swift, and Objective-C (for iOS software development).

How Pricey Are We?

As of 17/05/2025, JD Development & Designs has been a completely free company on the platform. However, at some point, we may have to begin charging for certain scripts or livery packages, depending on their value. I strive to provide for the community without the hassle. When we do start charging, payment will only be accepted through PayPal and Stripe, with more options possible in the future.

Careers at JD Development & Designs:

We offer unique, voluntary positions within the company, such as Human Resources and Community Engagement teams, to help grow and advertise JD Development & Designs. Higher positions are available for qualified candidates with proof of qualifications, especially within the HR and Community Engagement departments.

Who We Look For:

We're looking for people willing to engage with the FiveM community, build connections, and help both the company and our communities grow. If you're skilled in content creation (like FiveM media/art), community engagement, or coordination—even in challenging circumstances—and are at least 15-16 years old, you might be a perfect fit for our team! All positions are voluntary and a chance to help us succeed together.
